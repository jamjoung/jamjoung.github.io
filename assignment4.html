Challege 5: Accessibility Concerns

Definitely one accessibility issue arises with Challenge 2, where the chessboard pieces may be too small for visually impaired users to clearly see. Some of the colors have been swapped to be visible against the same color spaces (black chess piece on black space) so that may also confuse screen readers if read out loud. A solution to this would be making the board bigger and using actual images of the chess pieces that are easier to distinguish rather than unicode.

Another accessibility concern is with the form, as screen readers may have trouble with the labels and inputs. A solution to this would be to ensure that additional help text such as id, labels, etc are all included and also straight to the point so it's easy to understand.
